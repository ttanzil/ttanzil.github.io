About Me





I'm currently an undergraduate student at the Sophie Davis School of Biomedical Education's Accelerated BS/MD Program. I was previously New Hyde Park Memorial High School's Class of 2023 Salutatorian with a GPA of 103, SAT score of 1540 (800 Math & 740 English), and a perfect score of 36 on my ACT. I've also tutored Regents level subjects in person previously as a job and as a volunteer at my high school. You can read about more of my accolades by clicking here to open my resume.

I'm available to tutor Math and English grades 4 to 8. For High School, I'm also able to tutor Math and English, as well as the following Regents subjects: English, Algebra 1, Algebra 2, Earth Science, Living Environments, and Geometry. I also offer SAT, SHSAT, ACT, and PSAT tutoring, as well as the following AP Subjects: AP Calculus BC, AP Calculus AB, AP World History, AP Statistics, AP Psychology, and AP Biology. I also offer tutoring for college intro courses such as Chemistry 101, Biology 101, and College Algebra 1/2
